Temproral diversity In Kaski

Remote sensing plays a key role is in the study of land cover change. Specially, the conversion of natural land cover into human-dominated cover types continues to be a change of global proportions with many unknown environmental consequences. Result in visual form is used to remotely manage the desired area. Finally we discuss possible direction for the systematic arrangement of different classified elements such as vegetation, settlement area & mainly future planning for the urban areas.

Analysis of 2072 Earthquake

Nepal lies in the central part of the Himalayan chain and seismically active zone. Geologically, the Gorkha earthquake which is Mw 7.8 occurred on or close to the main Himalayan frontal thrust fault and geographically near Barpak, a mountain village of Gorkha district The earthquake was followed by many powerful aftershocks. From the graduated symbol map showing magnitude of earthquakes, we can analyse about the intensity of earthquake graphically, predict the damages caused.
